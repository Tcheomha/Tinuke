What We Do

We are a foundation focused on Girl-child education especially in Sciences, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM).

How We Operate

From time to time we organise boot camps for girls to learn science oriented courses. we conduct aptitude tests to select our participants from all over the country. We also sponsor high flying and achieving girls to study sciences in the university .
